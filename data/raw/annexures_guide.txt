Passport Annexures - Complete Guide

What are Annexures?
Annexures are self-declaration or verification forms required alongside the passport application in specific situations.

ANNEXURE A - Declaration of Emergency Contact and Identity
- Purpose: Provides emergency contact details and a self-declaration of identity
- Required by: All applicants (part of online application form now)

ANNEXURE B - Verification Certificate (Government Servants)
- Purpose: Identity and Address Verification Certificate for Government servants
- Required by: Government employees applying for a passport
- Who signs: Head of Office / authorized signatory

ANNEXURE C - No Objection Certificate for Government Servants
- Purpose: NOC from employer for government servants to obtain passport
- Required by: Government employees, including Central/State/PSU/Armed Forces
- Note: Without Annexure C, government employees cannot be issued a passport

ANNEXURE D - Declaration of Parent/Guardian for Minor Passport
- Purpose: Declaration by the applying parent AND consent of the other parent
- Required by: When only one parent is present at PSK for a minor's passport
- Must be notarized or sworn before a first-class magistrate

ANNEXURE E - Declaration for Change in Appearance
- Purpose: Self-declaration when there is a significant change in appearance
- Required when re-issuing a passport after surgery, major weight change, aging

ANNEXURE F - Verification Certificate by a Known Person for Tatkal
- Purpose: Identity verification certificate for Tatkal passport applications
- Acceptable signatories: Gazetted officer, MLA/MP, bank manager, doctor, lawyer, CA

ANNEXURE G - Declaration for Passport of a Child Born Out of Wedlock
- Purpose: Self-declaration for passport of a child born out of wedlock
- Must be sworn before a first-class magistrate or notary

ANNEXURE H - Non-Availability of Consent of the Other Parent
- Purpose: Declaration when consent of one parent is not available for minor's passport
- Common situations: Separated/divorced parents, single-parent families

ANNEXURE I - Declaration for Lost Passport
- Purpose: Self-declaration for passport lost or stolen
- Must be accompanied by FIR from local police station
- Lost passport fee is higher than normal re-issue fee

ANNEXURE J - Declaration for Damaged Passport
- Purpose: Self-declaration for a passport that has been physically damaged
- Must be accompanied by the damaged passport

IMPORTANT NOTES
- All annexures available for download at passportindia.gov.in
- Most annexures must be on non-judicial stamp paper (Rs. 10 or Rs. 20)
- Notarization is required for Annexure D, G, H, I, and J
- Carry 2 copies of each annexure to the PSK
