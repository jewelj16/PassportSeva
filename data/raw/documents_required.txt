Documents Required for Indian Passport Application

SECTION 1: PROOF OF IDENTITY (Any ONE of the following)
1. Aadhaar Card (most preferred and widely accepted)
2. Voter ID Card (Election Photo Identity Card / EPIC)
3. PAN Card
4. Driving License
5. Ration Card with photograph
6. Bank/Post Office passbook with photo
7. Government-issued photo identity card
8. Arms License issued by a competent authority
9. Pension document with photograph

SECTION 2: PROOF OF ADDRESS (Any ONE of the following)
1. Aadhaar Card (serves as both identity and address proof)
2. Voter ID Card
3. Bank passbook / Bank statement (last 3 months)
4. Water bill / Telephone (landline) bill / Electricity bill / Gas connection bill (in applicant's name, not older than 3 months)
5. Income Tax Assessment Order
6. Registered rent agreement
7. Letter from employer on company letterhead (original)
8. Spouse's passport (first and last page copy) for change of address after marriage

SECTION 3: PROOF OF DATE OF BIRTH (Any ONE of the following)
1. Birth Certificate issued by the Registrar of Births and Deaths or the Municipal Corporation or any other prescribed authority
2. Transfer/School Leaving/Matriculation Certificate issued by the school last attended
3. Policy bond issued by Public Life Insurance companies having Date of Birth
4. Aadhaar Card/E-Aadhaar having Date of Birth
5. PAN Card (contains Date of Birth)
6. Voter ID Card (contains Date of Birth)
7. Driving License (contains Date of Birth)

Note on Date of Birth Proof: For applicants born on or after 26.01.1989, the birth certificate issued by the Registrar of Births & Deaths or the Municipal Corporation is MANDATORY (Government Order dated 2018). School leaving certificate alone is NOT sufficient for this age group.

SECTION 4: DOCUMENTS FOR MINOR PASSPORT
1. Birth Certificate (MANDATORY for all minors)
2. Aadhaar Card of the child (if available)
3. Aadhaar Card of both parents
4. Passport of parents (if applicable)
5. School bonafide certificate / ID card
6. Consent of both parents:
   - If both parents are present at PSK: both sign the application
   - If one parent applies: Annexure D (consent of other parent) OR Annexure H (if consent cannot be obtained)
7. For adopted children: legal adoption certificate

SECTION 5: DOCUMENTS FOR RE-ISSUE
1. Old passport (original + self-attested photocopy of first 2 and last 2 pages)
2. If passport is lost: FIR copy from local police station + Annexure I
3. If passport is damaged: damaged passport + explanation letter
4. For name change: Gazette notification / marriage certificate / deed poll
5. For address change: new address proof document

SECTION 6: DOCUMENTS FOR GOVERNMENT SERVANTS
1. Identity Certificate / No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the employer
2. Prior political clearance from MEA (for diplomatic/official passports)
3. Forwarding letter from the ministry / department

SECTION 7: PHOTOGRAPH SPECIFICATIONS
- Size: 2 inches x 2 inches (51 mm x 51 mm)
- Background: White or off-white (plain, no patterns)
- Pose: Front-facing, full face, open eyes
- Expression: Neutral, mouth closed
- Head covering: Not allowed unless for religious reasons
- Glasses: Remove glasses for the photo
- The photo should be recent (taken within last 3 months)
- Print quality must be high (no pixelation, proper contrast)
- Ears should be visible
- For infants: photo of child alone (no parent holding)

SECTION 8: GENERAL RULES
- All original documents must be presented at PSK for verification
- Self-attested photocopies (1 set) must be submitted at PSK
- Documents in languages other than Hindi/English must be accompanied by a notarized translation
- Laminated documents must be accompanied by an unlaminated copy
- DigiLocker documents are accepted at all PSKs (no physical copy needed)
