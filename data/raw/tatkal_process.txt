Tatkal Passport - Complete Guide

What is Tatkal Passport?
Tatkal passport is an urgent/expedited passport service offered by the Government of India for citizens who need a passport on an urgent basis. The word "Tatkal" means "immediate" in Hindi.

Who Can Apply for Tatkal Passport?
Any Indian citizen can apply for a Tatkal passport. It is available for both fresh passport applications and re-issue applications.

Processing Time for Tatkal Passport
- Tatkal applications are processed on priority at the Passport Seva Kendra
- Typical processing time: 1 to 3 working days after submitting application at PSK
- In genuine emergency cases, same-day processing may be available

Tatkal Fees
The Tatkal fee is charged IN ADDITION to the normal passport fee:
- 36-page booklet: Rs. 1,500 (normal) + Rs. 2,000 (Tatkal surcharge) = Rs. 3,500 total
- 60-page booklet: Rs. 2,000 (normal) + Rs. 2,000 (Tatkal surcharge) = Rs. 4,000 total
- Minor (below 18): Rs. 1,000 (normal) + Rs. 2,000 (Tatkal surcharge) = Rs. 3,000 total

Documents Required for Tatkal Passport (Additional)
In addition to normal passport documents, Tatkal applicants must provide ONE of the following:
1. Aadhaar Card
2. Voter ID Card (EPIC)
3. PAN Card
4. Verification Certificate from an employer of a reputed company (Annexure F)
5. Verification Certificate from an educational institution (for students)

Police Verification for Tatkal
- For Tatkal passports, police verification is typically initiated BEFORE passport issuance
- Pre-verification police report is obtained before printing the passport
- Additional post-issuance police verification may also be conducted
- If police report is adverse, the passport may be impounded

Tatkal Passport for Minors
- Same Tatkal surcharge applies for minors
- Both parents' consent is required (or Annexure D/H)
- Birth certificate is mandatory
- School bonafide certificate may be required

Emergency Certificate vs Tatkal
- Emergency Certificate: Issued by Indian missions abroad for emergency travel when regular passport is unavailable
- Tatkal Passport: Urgent passport issued within India through the regular passport system
- Emergency certificates are single-journey documents, not full passports

Important Notes
- Tatkal scheme is available at all PSKs across India
- You must book a Tatkal appointment specifically (not a normal appointment)
- There is no way to convert a normal application to Tatkal after submission
- If you need to change to Tatkal, you must submit a new application with Tatkal fee
- Tatkal passport has the same validity as normal passport (10 years for adults, 5 years for minors)
