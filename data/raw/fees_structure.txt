Passport Fee Structure - Government of India (Updated 2024)

FRESH PASSPORT FEES

1. Normal Fresh Passport (36 pages) - For applicants 18 years and above:
   - Fee: Rs. 1,500
   - Processing Time: 30-45 working days

2. Normal Fresh Passport (60 pages) - For frequent travelers:
   - Fee: Rs. 2,000
   - Processing Time: 30-45 working days

3. Fresh Passport for Minors (36 pages) - Below 18 years:
   - Fee: Rs. 1,000
   - Processing Time: 30-45 working days

TATKAL (URGENT) PASSPORT FEES

4. Tatkal Fresh Passport (36 pages) - Adults:
   - Normal Fee: Rs. 1,500 + Tatkal Fee: Rs. 2,000 = Total: Rs. 3,500
   - Processing Time: 1-3 working days

5. Tatkal Fresh Passport (60 pages) - Adults:
   - Normal Fee: Rs. 2,000 + Tatkal Fee: Rs. 2,000 = Total: Rs. 4,000
   - Processing Time: 1-3 working days

6. Tatkal Fresh Passport for Minors (36 pages):
   - Normal Fee: Rs. 1,000 + Tatkal Fee: Rs. 2,000 = Total: Rs. 3,000
   - Processing Time: 1-3 working days

RE-ISSUE PASSPORT FEES

7. Re-issue of Passport (36 pages) - Normal:
   - Fee: Rs. 1,500
   - Processing Time: 30-45 working days

8. Re-issue of Passport (60 pages) - Normal:
   - Fee: Rs. 2,000
   - Processing Time: 30-45 working days

9. Re-issue of Passport (36 pages) - Tatkal:
   - Normal Fee: Rs. 1,500 + Tatkal Fee: Rs. 2,000 = Total: Rs. 3,500
   - Processing Time: 1-3 working days

OTHER FEES

10. Duplicate Passport (in lieu of lost/damaged):
    - Fee: Rs. 3,000 (36 pages) or Rs. 3,500 (60 pages)
    - Additional penalty may apply if passport was lost due to negligence

11. Police Clearance Certificate (PCC):
    - Fee: Rs. 500
    - Used for immigration purposes to countries requiring PCC

12. Identity Certificate:
    - Fee: Rs. 100
    - For emergency travel document

13. Emergency Certificate (issued by Indian missions abroad):
    - Fee: Varies by mission
    - For emergency travel back to India

PAYMENT METHODS
- Online payment via Credit Card, Debit Card, Net Banking, or UPI
- SBI Bank Challan payment (offline option)
- Cash payment at PSK is NOT accepted
- Fee once paid is NON-REFUNDABLE even if the application is rejected or withdrawn

FEE EXEMPTIONS
- No fee exemptions exist for any category as per current rules
- Senior citizens pay the same fee as other adults
- Government servants pay the same fee (department may reimburse)

ADDITIONAL CHARGES (NOT included in passport fee)
- Speed Post charges for passport delivery: included in the fee
- Police verification: No separate fee charged to the applicant
- SMS alerts: No separate charge
- Photocopying at PSK: May be charged separately (Rs. 2-5 per page at PSK)

IMPORTANT NOTES
- All fees are subject to change by Government notification
- Check passportindia.gov.in for the latest fee schedule
- GST is NOT applicable on passport fees (government service)
- Fee for Tatkal is the same regardless of the reason for urgency
